About Us

It is about finding the right sound for our customers here at Sounds of the Times Studios. My 8 years experience in the music industry has had me work some top musicians in the industry. I have recorded in top studios from Ghana, Africa to Berlin and Leipzig Germany through which I have gained much experience and learnt many tricks from the best producers and engineers in Germany. We have created over the years audio projects such as animation sounds, documentaries, song EPs and LPs and original compositions of music for all kinds of media. Contact us if you are interested in our offers, for we are here to turn your music into masterpieces.
